Our Services

Morumiwa specializes in running errands for individuals and hospitals. Explore our tailored services below to find the perfect solution for your needs.

Personal Errands: "For Your Everyday Life"

This is for individuals and families who need to save time or require assistance due to mobility, illness, or a busy schedule.

  • Medicine & Prescription: Collection from the pharmacy and delivery to your home.
  • Retail Exchanges & Returns: Taking items back to stores for returns or size exchanges
  • Queueing Service: Standing in line for you at government departments, ticket offices, or popular stores.

Business Errands: "Your Business Support Partner"

This B2B service is aimed at small to medium-sized businesses that need a reliable partner to handle operational tasks, freeing them up to focus on their core activities.

  • Event & Staff Support: Providing an extra set of hands for short-staffed businesses during events (e.g. catering, décor setup).
  • Local Courier Service: Urgent, same-day delivery of documents, small packages, or products to clients.
  • Post-Box Clearing: Regular collection of mail from the company’s post-box.

Institutional Support: "Care & Comfort Services"

This specialised service focuses on providing a compassionate, human connection for people in hospitals, clinics, retirement communities, or student residences.

  • Home-to-Hospital Delivery: Delivering warm meals, specific toiletries, phone chargers, books, blankets, and other personal items from home.
  • Entertainment: Picking up magazines, newspapers, puzzle books, or dropping off/charging devices.
  • Gift & Flower Delivery: Delivering flowers, balloons, or gifts from friends and family who can't visit.

Premium Services: "The Executive Assistant"

This is a high-trust, high-value category for critical or sensitive tasks that require the utmost professionalism, discretion, and reliability. These services likely command a higher price point.

  • Home & Pet Sitting Coordination: Being the point of contact for a house sitter or checking in on a property for clients who are travelling.
  • Property Management Support: Key handover services for rental properties or Airbnbs, and conducting basic entry/exit inspections.
  • Vehicle Maintenance: Taking a client's car for its annual service, a tyre change, or for a car wash and detailing.
