About Us

Your Time is Your Priority. Making it Happen is Ours.

Ever wished you could clone yourself just to get everything done?

We give you the freedom to be productive at work while we take care of your to-do list.

With Morumiwa The Errand Guys, you're not just delegating a task; you're investing in efficiency and peace of mind.

Let us handle the running around, so you can get back to what you do best.

Our values are the principles that guide every action we take and the foundation of our promise to our clients and our community.

Empathy

Community

Innovation

We constantly seek new ways to bridge the gaps our clients face, using strategic partnerships and a problem-solving mindset.

Bridging the Gap

In today's fast-paced world, individuals and organizations face a fundamental challenge: the impossibility of being in two places at once. This creates a significant gap between what needs to be done and the time and physical capacity to do it, leading to lost opportunities and added stress. Morumiwa is dedicated to solving this problem.

For Individuals

We bridge the gap between your busy schedule and essential personal errands, so you don't have to choose between work and life.

For Businesses

We close the gap that exists when your staff is needed for a task that distracts them from their core functions.

For Hospitals

We bridge the critical gap when patients, families, and staff are unable to leave the hospital to manage personal needs.
